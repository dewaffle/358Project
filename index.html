Project Idea

Heir is a website idea that helps people understand how they are connected within a family, group, or organization. It presents relationships in a structured way so users can easily follow connections and roles.

The project started from the problem that many family members become less connected over time and may not clearly know how they are related anymore. The same structure can also be useful in companies, teams, and communities.
