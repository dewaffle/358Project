About the Project

Heir is a project that focuses on building a structured digital view of relationships between people. It helps users understand how individuals belong to a family, team, or organization.

The project combines social value with practical organization.

Our Goals

  1. Improve understanding of relationships
  2. Support continuity across generations and groups
  3. Provide a simple and clear structure
